What makes Attune different

Not another generic wellness app. Every recommendation is backed by peer-reviewed research and personalized to your biometrics.

🧬

Biometric Intelligence

We read your HRV, resting heart rate, body temperature, sleep stages, and activity — then cross-reference against published research to find what actually works for your body.

🍽

Precision Nutrition

Meal suggestions mapped to your recovery state. Low HRV? Anti-inflammatory foods. Poor deep sleep? Magnesium-rich dinners. Every recommendation includes the study behind it.

🏋

Adaptive Movement

Training recommendations that flex with your readiness score. Zone 2 on recovery days, high intensity when your body is primed. No more guessing.

🧘

Recovery Protocols

Breathwork, cold/heat exposure, stretching routines, and relaxation techniques matched to your nervous system state. Parasympathetic support when you need it.

📈

Trend Tracking

Watch how your interventions perform over weeks. Did the magnesium actually improve deep sleep? Did the Zone 2 cardio boost HRV? Data doesn't lie.

🔒

Your Data, Your Control

All processing happens securely. We never sell your data. Export everything anytime. Full transparency, always.
